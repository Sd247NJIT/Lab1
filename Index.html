Intro to Nissan GTR Skyline: The R34



Japanese cars are highly reliable and they are not required for high maintenance. One of the best JDM are the collection of the Nissan Skyline GT-R cars, which came around the late 1960s. It has been considered one the best sports cars from the 1990s. Specifically, the Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is one of the most popular high-performance sports cars among other Skylines (such as the R31, R32, and R35) within JDMs (Japanese Domestic Market). The R34 is a two-door sports car, with the driver's wheel being on the right side. It has a Twin Turbo engine bay with the Inline-six motor, which is rated to run at 280 horsepower.

Fun fact: The car gained more recognition in The Fast and Furious movie franchise. The car was first featured in the 2 Fast 2 Furious movie, driven by Paul Walker in the Miami scene. Click here for more information on Paul Walker's car.
